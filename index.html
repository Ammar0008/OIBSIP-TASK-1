About John Cena

John Cena is an American professional wrestler, actor, and television presenter. Born on April 23, 1977, in West Newbury, Massachusetts, Cena has become one of the most recognizable faces in sports entertainment and beyond.

Career Highlights

  • 16-time world champion in WWE
  • 5-time United States Champion
  • 4-time world tag team champion
  • 2012 Money in the Bank ladder match winner
  • 2-time Royal Rumble winner (2008, 2013)

Beyond Wrestling

John Cena has also made a name for himself outside the wrestling ring. He has starred in several Hollywood films, including "Trainwreck," "Bumblebee," and "F9." Cena is also known for his philanthropic work, having granted over 650 wishes for the Make-A-Wish Foundation, more than any other celebrity.
